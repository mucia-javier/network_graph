To close this sidebar, just click on the × on the upper right corner and get back graphing space.

This is where the values of the arguments will be changed. Currently only the name/label of a node (or edge) can be changed. To do so, a node (or an edge) must be selected/clicked before editing the 'value' field in this sidebar.

Remember that you can zoom in the graph by scrolling DOWN with two fingers (on your touchpad) and zoom out by scrolling UP with two fingers. You can also move the graph around by clicking on any area around the graph that is not an edge or a node and dragging the graph around.

Side Note: This sidebar is scrollable, which means that it can hold as much content as it needs to.
